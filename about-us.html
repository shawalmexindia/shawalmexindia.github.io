About Us

ALMEX GROUP

In 1962, the patented Almex pressure bag principle revolutionized press design worldwide and positioned Shaw Almex as an industry leader. Innovative Almex presses provide uniform temperature and pressure required to cure, laminate, vulcanize or mold products for a wide array of industries.

Nine corporate facilities and a network of exclusive distributors are strategically located on five continents to provide a comprehensive sales and service support system to clients in over 120 countries. Shaw Almex demonstrates a social commitment to this global community by contributing to local charitable causes and adhering to stringent environmental standards.

The Shaw Almex tradition of quality lives on through the Almex Group of companies, the unique Almex presses, complementary Fusion products, and a profound commitment to excellence, integrity, and social responsibility in all aspects of business endeavors around the world.

SHAW ALMEX INDIA

Shaw Almex India Private Limited, is one of the leading manufacturers and suppliers of belt vulcanizers and transmission belt press, established in the year 1989, at Faridabad, Haryana, India. Our complete product line includes svp sectional vulcanizing press, membrane press, membrane press, vacuum membrane press, thermofoil membrane press and vacuum press.

Our business operations are led by Mr. B. Dutta, who has with him extensive business experience in the field of manufacturing of industrial products like fabric belt vulcanizer, transmission belt press, PVC belt vulcanizer, PU belt vulcanizer and others. Further, the strong service support provided by our experienced team of professionals allow us to successfully handle the supply demands of steelcord & fabric belt vulcanizer that are suitable for belt width up to 2400mm; PVC / PU belt vulcanizer up to 3500mm & industrial presses up to 6500mm long & 2600 mm wide. Further, we also hold expertise in offering a wide range of conveyor Belt, carry out repair of vulcanizers as well as custom design the presses for meeting the demands of unique industrial applications.

Today, we work on streamlined manufacturing processes in order to achieve high production rates. Our extensive R & D work also assists us in keeping ourselves abreast with the upcoming changes in the technological advancements, which assists us in bringing constant changes and modifications in the offered product range. Further, the superior manufacturing support and stringent quality control practiced at our end has also helped us in making many quality conscious clients in the global markets including that of China, Indonesia, Singapore, Taiwan and North America.
